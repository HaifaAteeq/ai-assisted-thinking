# Setup Guide

## Quick Start (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Choose Your AI
- Claude (recommended)
- ChatGPT Plus
- Other AI chatbot

### Step 2: Copy a Prompt
Go to `/prompts/` and copy one:
- `system_prompt_v2.2.md` - General learning
- `math_for_ml_prompt.md` - Math for ML
- `academic_reading_writing_prompt.md` - Paper reading

### Step 3: Paste and Start
1. Open your AI chatbot
2. Paste the entire prompt
3. Say what you want to learn
4. AI will guide you

## Tips

- Start with energy level 3+
- Have 30-60 minutes available
- Be honest about confusion
- Demonstrate, don't just claim understanding

## Session Logs

After each session, document your learning in `/sessions/`
Use the template in `/templates/template_session.md`
