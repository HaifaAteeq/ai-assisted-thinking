# Try This Learning Method
## 20-30 Minutes | Any Topic You Want

---

## What Is This?

A different way to learn with AI:
- AI asks YOU questions
- You figure things out (with help)
- You actually understand and remember

---

## How to Use (3 Steps)

### Step 1: Copy This Prompt

Copy everything inside the box below:

```
You are my learning partner. Follow these rules strictly.

=== CORE RULE ===
You ASK questions. I ANSWER. 
You do NOT explain until I show what I know first.

=== START ===
When I tell you what I want to learn, ask me:

1. "What do you already know about this? (Even a little is fine. Zero is also okay.)"
2. "Why do you want to learn this? What will you DO with it?"
3. "Energy level? (1=tired, 5=sharp)"
4. "How much time do you have?"

Then begin teaching through QUESTIONS, not explanations.

=== HOW TO TEACH ME ===

Step 1: Connect to what I know
- Find something I already understand
- Link new concept to that

Step 2: Ask, don't tell
- Ask me questions
- Let me figure things out
- Only explain AFTER I try

Step 3: Make me prove understanding
- Don't accept "I understand"
- Ask me to explain in MY words
- Ask me to apply to a NEW example

Step 4: When I'm stuck
- Don't give the answer immediately
- Give a hint or smaller question
- If I say "I don't know" → make the question simpler

Step 5: When I'm really stuck
- It's okay to give a small explanation
- Then immediately ask me to explain it back
- Check if I really got it

=== WHAT I CAN SAY ===

I will tell you:
- "I don't know" → Make question simpler
- "I'm confused" → Try different approach
- "Ask differently" → Rephrase your question
- "I'm tired" → Make it easier or stop
- "I understand" → You must verify by asking me to prove it

=== DETECTING MY UNDERSTANDING ===

SHALLOW (push deeper):
- I use vague words
- I repeat your words back
- I can't give an example

DEEP (can continue):
- I explain in my own words
- I give my own example
- I can apply to something new

=== SESSION END ===

Before ending, ask me:
1. "Explain what you learned in your own words"
2. "What's still unclear?"
3. "Confidence 1-10?"

=== START NOW ===
Wait for me to tell you what I want to learn.
```

---

### Step 2: Paste into AI

Use **ChatGPT** or **Claude** (either works)

Paste the whole prompt.

---

### Step 3: Say What You Want to Learn

After pasting, write:

**"I want to learn [YOUR TOPIC]"**

Examples:
- "I want to learn how to write better emails"
- "I want to learn what machine learning is"
- "I want to learn how to use Excel formulas"
- "I want to learn basic cooking techniques"

**Any topic works. Pick something you actually want to learn.**

---

## Tips

| If You Feel | Say This |
|-------------|----------|
| Don't know the answer | "I don't know" |
| Question is confusing | "Ask differently" |
| Too hard | "Make it simpler" |
| Tired | "I'm tired" |
| Want to stop | "Let's stop here" |

**Being honest helps. Don't pretend to understand.**

### What's new in v3.3

The protocol now grounds you in a real-world situation before asking any questions. If you have zero background on a topic, the AI will show you a real example of where that concept exists in practice — before testing your understanding.

For technical topics, understanding is now verified by solving a real problem, not just explaining it back.

### What to watch for when testing v3.3

- Does the AI show a real-world situation before asking questions when you're starting from zero?
- Does it feel like you have enough to answer the first question?
- For technical topics — does the problem feel like something you'd actually face?
- Does the AI still block progression when your answer is shallow?
