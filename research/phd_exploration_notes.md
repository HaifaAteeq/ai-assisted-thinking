## Research Question Draft

### Current Direction

> "How can AI-assisted learning protocols improve genuine understanding verification in self-directed learning, and what design principles ensure learners demonstrate rather than claim comprehension?"

### Sub-questions

1. What protocol features effectively detect shallow vs. deep understanding?
2. How do recovery mechanisms affect learner persistence and outcomes?
3. What role does domain knowledge play in AI tutoring effectiveness?
4. How does forced articulation impact long-term retention?
